Our story

Mechanics who actually give a damn

PivotPoint Auto started in 2009 when brothers Jake and Matt Henare decided Wellington deserved a workshop where you're treated like a neighbour, not a number.

We're a small team of five — each with a genuine passion for keeping cars on the road. We explain everything in plain English, never push unnecessary work, and stand behind every job we do.
